## Paragraph
a line of text followed by one or more blank line.

## Headings
lines begin with several `#`

## Blockquotes
lines begin with `>`

## Styling Text
texts wrapped in `*` will be italic, text wrapped in `**` will be bold

## Lists
### Unordered Lists
lines begin with `*` or `-`

### Ordered Lists
lines begin with `1.`

### Nested Lists
lines begin with `  1.`

## Code
### Inline Format
texts wrapped in \`

### Multiple Lines
texts wrapped in \`\`\`
or \`\`\`javascript
